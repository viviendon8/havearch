About

HAVE Studio was founded in 2019 by principal designers Yuanyuan Dong, Yue He, and Nuo Xu, whose shared passion for architecture and urban planning was shaped through their studies at USC and Columbia University. Initially rooted in conceptual design competitions, the studio has since expanded its practice to focus on residential architecture, resort hotels, and installations. Operating across New York and Shanghai, HAVE Studio bridges experimental design thinking with practical construction, delivering projects that balance creativity, context, and buildability.
